Adminner is a small, fast, reliable and easy-to-use tool for administrators to make their routine work much easier. It is agent-less, which means that no software needs to be installed on the network computers for full control. It can manage all computers, services, programs, files, accounts, groups in your LAN on one computer, and need not to login logout one by one computer on remote desktop!

It's the helpful tool for the administrators who have lots of managed hosts.

It's useful when you have more services or programs to manage in the local intranet or the equipment room, even without active directory.

Adminner is designed to help reduce costs, increase efficiency and reduce errors!

It doesn't need domain server or Active Directory. You can work more easily in workgroup.

 

 

Adminner is for those who need to

  • manage several servers or workstations in the LAN.
  • manage services or programs in several computers in the LAN.
  • start, stop, restart services frequently.
  • start, stop restart services in the LAN on time.
  • run programs on the computers in the LAN on time.
  • frequently create, update, delete files in the LAN.
  • frequently modify the connection string or other texts in the LAN.
  • manage user account or groups on the computers in the LAN easily but dont have active directory and domain server.
